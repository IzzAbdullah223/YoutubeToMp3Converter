How to download YouTube videos
  1. Go to YouTube.com and search for a video you would like to download. Then copy the video link from the browser address bar (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ExampleID).
  2. Paste the YouTube video link in the Convertor
  3. The convertor will start downloading the video. Please note that the download may take time depending on the speed of your WIFI or video length.
